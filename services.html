Our Services

What our management covers

We provide end-to-end property management for owners across Asia who invest in Thai real estate. From listing your villa on global platforms to keeping your pool crystal clear while you're a thousand kilometres away — every detail is handled so you don't have to be.

📊 Revenue & Guests

Short-Term Rentals

We manage your property's presence across Airbnb, Booking.com, and Agoda — creating optimised listings with professional photography, compelling descriptions in multiple languages, and strategic positioning to reach guests from across Asia and beyond.

Guest communication is handled end-to-end in English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and Thai. From initial enquiry through check-in coordination to post-stay reviews, every touchpoint is covered. We respond within the hour, every day of the year.

Dynamic pricing algorithms adjust your nightly rate based on seasonality, local events, competitor analysis, and occupancy trends. You receive detailed monthly reports with revenue breakdowns, occupancy data, and market benchmarks — delivered in your preferred language.

🏡 Maintenance & Upkeep

Property Care

Thailand's tropical climate demands consistent, proactive maintenance. Our team schedules regular pool cleaning, garden upkeep, AC servicing, pest control, and plumbing inspections — preventing small issues from becoming costly problems while you're away.

Every visit produces a detailed inspection report with timestamped photos, sent directly to your phone or email. You'll know the exact condition of your property at all times, whether you're in Shanghai, Tokyo, or Seoul.

For urgent issues — a burst pipe during monsoon season, a power outage before a guest arrival — our local team responds within hours. We maintain relationships with trusted contractors across all four of our service regions to ensure fast, fair-priced repairs.

📡 Smart Home & Security

Remote Monitoring

Smart lock installation gives you and your guests keyless entry — no more lost keys, no more locksmith calls. Each guest receives a unique access code that expires automatically at checkout, and you can grant or revoke access from anywhere in the world.

Security cameras cover exterior areas and common spaces, with footage accessible through a secure app on your phone. Climate sensors monitor temperature and humidity to protect your furnishings and alert you to unusual conditions — particularly useful during Thailand's rainy season.

All alerts are delivered in real time. Water leak detected at 2 AM? You'll know immediately, and our local team will already be on the way. The system integrates seamlessly with your existing property setup, and we handle all installation and configuration.

✨ Housekeeping & Turnover

Cleaning & Turnover

Every guest turnover follows a hotel-standard checklist: fresh linens, spotless bathrooms, restocked amenities, and a final walk-through inspection before the next arrival. Our cleaning teams are trained to the standards expected by international travellers who leave reviews.

Linen replacement uses premium, hotel-grade sheets and towels. We manage the full laundry cycle — pickup, professional washing, pressing, and return — so your property always presents at its best. Worn items are flagged and replaced before they affect guest perception.

Deep cleaning services are available on a scheduled basis or on demand. This includes full kitchen degreasing, grout restoration, upholstery treatment, and mattress sanitisation. For properties with pools, we coordinate cleaning schedules with pool maintenance to minimise disruption.

🔍 Acquisition Support

Finding Your Place

Before you invest, you need data — not sales pitches. We provide independent market analysis across Phuket, Koh Samui, Bangkok, and Chiang Mai, comparing rental yields, occupancy rates, capital appreciation trends, and total cost of ownership in each area.

Our area comparison reports factor in the details that matter to remote owners: proximity to airports, quality of local contractors, strength of the rental market in low season, and the specific regulations that apply to foreign ownership in each location.

Once you've identified your target area, we assist with property sourcing, developer due diligence, and connecting you with trusted legal professionals who specialise in Thai property law for foreign buyers. We don't earn commissions from developers — our advice is independent.

🎨 Design & Furnishing

Interior Styling

Properties with professional styling consistently outperform unfurnished or poorly furnished listings. Higher search rankings, better photos, more bookings, and stronger reviews — the investment pays for itself within the first few months of rental income.

Our process begins with an on-site assessment and a mood board proposal tailored to your property's architecture, target guest demographic, and budget. We source furniture, decor, lighting, and fixtures locally from Thai artisans and suppliers, keeping costs reasonable while achieving a look that photographs beautifully.

Full installation is handled by our team, including furniture assembly, art hanging, styling of decorative elements, and a professional photo shoot of the finished space. The entire process — from initial assessment to completed installation — typically takes 2 to 4 weeks.
